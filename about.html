Who We Are

AP Textile Industries: Panipat's Trusted Yarn Exporter

AP Textile Industries was established in 2010 in Panipat, Haryana. Over the past decade, we have grown from a local yarn trading unit into a leading bulk manufacturer and exporter. Our key milestones include:

  • 2010 Foundation: Founded in Panipat, sourcing premium Indian cotton and polyester yarn for local buyers.
  • Product Expansion: Expanded our wholesale portfolio to include Malai Dori (satin cord yarn) for specialty crafts.
  • Home Décor Line: Launched a hospitality-grade home décor line featuring 100% cotton bath towels and anti-slip bath mats.
  • Global Reach: Currently supplying 200+ businesses across 10+ Indian states, with expanding exports to Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

Our core strength lies in building lasting relationships—with yarn mills, B2B buyers, and global textile importers.
